My university notes

This is a collection of my university notes. Feel free to use them for your own studies! Note that they are in Italian, but I may translate them in English some day... These notes were taken using Notion, a free and very powerful tool that I use for almost every organisation task (not sponsored, although I wish I was). Their pro subscription is free for students, you just have to sign in with your university email and opt in through Notion's settings. Give it a try. It has made my life so much simpler!
